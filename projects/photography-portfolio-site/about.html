About Jim

Jim Strickland is a city photographer with 15 years of experience of photography.

Born and raised in Orlando Florida, He went to college for photography and got two Degrees. Loving photography, Jim started his career when he was in high school, taking pictures of street views around his neighborhood and editing the images. Taking photoshop courses as well to make detailed edits to his images. His work has landed in some magazines and covers around the world.
